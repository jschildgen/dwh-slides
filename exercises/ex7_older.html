Exercise 1: MERGE

In the next workbook (Challenge 4), there will only be a short question about this exercise sheet.

On the previous exercise sheet, you integrated data from your staging area into your target data-warehouse schema. Now, extend this workflow by writing a MERGE command. MERGE is a combination of INSERT, UPDATE, and DELETE. Use it to insert rows from source tables from your staging area into target tables that are not there yet, and update those rows which already exist in the target table. You can also use multiple MERGE commands to integrate data from different source tables into the same target table.
