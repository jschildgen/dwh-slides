Exercise 3: Report

Create a Power BI report that shows the results of your project: interesting facts and findings; do not focus on how you did it.

  1. Create report that consists 1 or multiple pages. Mind that you have only 5 minutes time to present your report.
  2. Use different visualizations (e.g., bar chart, line chart, pie chart, table, map, slicer, filter, ...).
  3. Please write your title and your name on the top of your first page.
  4. Export your report as a PDF: File, Export, Export to PDF.
  5. Choose your presentation slot in ELO and Upload your PDF to ELO before the deadline (July 1, 23:59).
  6. If you want, you can also upload your pbix file to ELO. This allows you to use the interactive elements in your presentation.
  7. All PDFs and pbix files will be available on the professor's presentation PC. Alternatively, you can also use your own notebook for your presentation.
